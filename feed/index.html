This was our first conference, and we wanted to put something together that was both awesome and memorable, hence wanting to craft a more permanent reminder of the event for those who contributed in whatever way possible.

Every speaker, sponsor and volunteer received one of our custom minted challenge coins, featuring the conference details, and Perth’s very recognisable skyline, adapted from a photo taken by nidogski

Doles takes the credit for the final design…and guess we now need to up our game for 2018!
